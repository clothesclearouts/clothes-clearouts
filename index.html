❓ FAQs

Where do you collect from? ▾
We're based in Watford and collect from Watford and the surrounding areas. If you're not sure whether you're too far, just drop us a message - we cover a wider area than you might think and we'll always do our best to help. We've collected from as far as 12 miles away before!
How quickly do you collect? ▾
We aim to be as flexible as possible! We often collect same day or next day, so if you want things gone quickly just let us know when you get in touch and we'll do our best to make it happen.
How much will I get? ▾
We pay 60p per kilo, weighed at collection. So the more you have, the more you earn! A typical black bin bag of clothes weighs around 8–12kg, so you could be looking at £5–£7 per bag. We weigh everything in front of you so it's completely transparent.
How do you pay? ▾
We pay instantly via PayPal or bank transfer - whichever works best for you. Payment is sent as soon as we've weighed your clothes, so you won't be waiting around.
What condition do clothes need to be in? ▾
Clothes need to be clean and in wearable condition - think items you'd be happy to see someone else wearing. We can't take anything stained, damaged, or heavily worn. We also don't take underwear, swimwear, socks, or workwear/uniforms. If you're unsure, just send us a photo and we'll let you know!
Is there a minimum amount? ▾
There's no strict minimum, but to make the trip worthwhile we'd generally recommend having at least a couple of bags ready. Drop us a message and we can chat through what you've got!
Do I need to do anything to prepare? ▾
Just bag everything up in black bin bags and leave them ready for collection. No need to sort, fold or organise - we keep it as easy as possible for you!
